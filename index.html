Tokenomics

BSC Contract Address: 0x7e7d2b9acc8953c92912d5abc5e39b7105e18c3a

Liquidity Locked, .1 inital LP Microcap, deflationary (a portion of every transaction is burned), and fair launched.

Pyramid is a BEP-20 token running on the Binance Smart Chain Network. It functions similarly to other BEP-20 tokens, however the transfer function within the smart contract makes it unique (e.g., liquidity pool acquisition and token burn.)

All token transfers incur a 12% fee. (this will be set lower at a later date)

2% goes towards liquidity pool acquisition. This fee is broken down to .5% 100X and .5% BNB which provides additional liquidity and overall health of the Pancake Swap 100X/BNB trading pair.

6% goes towards a token burn, which is where tokens are sent to the DEAD address forever lowering the supply of 100x

4% goes towards marketing and community development. Advertisements, influencers, contests and promotions
